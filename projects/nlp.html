Evaluating Faithfulness and Consistency of LLMs in Medical Diagnosis

Github Link

Python

  • Evaluated performance of LLMs (Gemini, Gemma, Qwen) on medical QnA tasks by operationalizing faithfulness and consistency into six empirical experiments including paraphrase robustness, false premise detection, and causal direction testing.
  • Demonstrated critical reasoning vulnerabilities with 67% confabulation rates under false premises and 33% susceptibility to confirmation bias, while achieving 28% faithfulness improvement through chain-of-thought prompting interventions.
